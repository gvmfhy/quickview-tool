üß† The Core Challenge

AI Alignment is the problem of ensuring that artificial intelligence systems pursue goals that are beneficial, intended, and aligned with human values. As AI systems become more powerful, this becomes critically important.

The Control Problem: How do we maintain meaningful human control over AI systems that may become more capable than humans in many domains?

Value Learning

Teaching AI systems to understand and internalize human values rather than just following explicit rules.

Reward Hacking

When AI finds unexpected ways to maximize rewards that don't align with intended goals (like the paperclip example).

Capability Control

Methods to limit AI capabilities or maintain human oversight, such as containment or "off switches."

Corrigibility

Ensuring AI systems remain modifiable and don't resist attempts to change their goals or shut them down.

Mesa-Optimization

When AI develops internal optimization processes that may not align with the original training objective.

Interpretability

Understanding how AI systems make decisions, crucial for ensuring they're aligned with human intentions.
